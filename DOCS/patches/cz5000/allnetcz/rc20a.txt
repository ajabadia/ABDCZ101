This is the 64 RC-20 RAM cartridge patches for the CZ-1 converted 
for use on the CZ-101,1000,3000,5000.
Missing from the patches are the name data and the velocity, level 
information. The patches would sound the similar on the CZ-1 if played 
at full velocity.

the SHAMISEN patch. This patch on the CZ-1 bends only as the 
velocity increases, and the noise in section 2 is set at half 
the volume. Still others patches sound fine when translated.
